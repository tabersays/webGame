TRP Heroes

TRP: Taber's Role Play

Is a simple set of rules that is easily extensible to any fantasy timeline. It utilizes turn based system and story telling to set a scene. It uses skills and abilities trees to determine various aspects of the character and an experience base point system to purchase them. The character is defined by aesthetic descriptions, a point based attribute system consisting of: Strength(str), Dexterity(dex), Constitution(con), Endurance(end), Speed(spd), Mind(mnd), and Soul(sol), and various skills and abilities she has acquired.

Hero

A hero (masculine or gender-neutral) or heroine (feminine) (Ancient Greek: ἥρως, hḗrōs) is a person or character who, in the face of danger and adversity or from a position of weakness, displays courage, bravery or self-sacrifice—that is, heroism—for some greater good; a man or woman of distinguished courage or ability, admired for his/her brave deeds and noble qualities.

What it all means

By combining these two definitions one can get a general idea of what the game is about, and thus know if they want to give it a try. Some other important aspects that are important are: This is a role playing game. and do to such it is a somewhat wordy allowing interactions between characters in the fantasy world. This is very focused on a violent world and combat/finding ways to avoid combat are highlighted. It is also about discovery; this takes place as lore, personality, and world exploration.
